About Me

I'm all about helping people and organizations grow—it's what drives me whether I'm implementing public policies or getting different stakeholders to work together. When it comes to project management, I have a knack for taking complicated policy ideas and turning them into real solutions that actually work, making sure everyone on the team understands what we're doing and why.

What really gets me excited is finding practical ways to solve tricky problems. I love creating products and experiences that make a genuine difference in communities. When I'm not working, you'll find me trying out recipes from around the world or catching a baseball game. These simple pleasures connect me to different cultures and communities in ways that feel meaningful to me. I'm always looking for those sweet spots where tech and human-centered design can make life better and more fair for everyone.
